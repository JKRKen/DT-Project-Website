The lyrics of the song Kamulah The Only tell about the complexity of a person's relationship with his God.

The song Kamulah satu-satunya by Dewa 19 has long been branded as a love song with a romantic meaning. Who would have thought that this song by Ahmad Dhani and Erwin Prasetya had a religious meaning.

If we pay attention to the lyrics, it means someone who is conveying his sincerity to God.

Not only is it sang to express feelings of love, but this song can also be used to contemplate the relationship between man and his God.
