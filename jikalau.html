This song tells about a man's struggle to gain the trust of his woman for a time that will be maximized for happiness together. Even though there are many trials and obstacles, he will still be optimistic to think in order to fulfill the promise he has made.

The song If is one of the most popular songs from the band Naif. The emotional lyrics describe the feelings of someone who loves someone but the love is not reciprocated. The bridge part of the song expresses the hope that a loved one will finally respond to his feelings. However, until now his love remains unrequited.

If you feel the same way, then give this song a listen and let the lyrics comfort your heart. And who knows, maybe one day the person you love will respond to your feelings.
