At that time MCPY was piggybacking his younger brother named MCS on a motorbike, when suddenly from the opposite direction suddenly appeared a car sliding past the road markings, then the crash was unavoidable. The car crashed into the motorcyclist.

MCPY died while being treated at the hospital after the accident. Head of Traffic Unit of Sukoharjo Police Betty Nugroho said that, “The car went too to the right, so that it exceeded the road line. The driver only paid little attention to the traffic in front of him. From the opposite direction the victim's motorbike appeared, causing the accident.”

From the results of the information obtained by the police from witnesses around the location, there were allegations that the driver of the car was driving drunk.
