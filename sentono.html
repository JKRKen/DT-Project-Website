Sentono Gentong Pacitan, The Attractions

Visitors arriving at Sentono Gentong Pacitan will enjoy an open view of the stunning Pacitan Bay. You can also see the white sand from Teleng Ria Beach to Pancer Door Beach which looks like a crescent moon.

The beauty of the scenery seems to blend with the rows of hills that surround it like a giant wall. The occasional wisps of thin clouds make the atmosphere more serene. Sentono Gentong Pacitan is also the venue for paragliding. Several paragliding competitions have been held in this place.

At night, visitors will be presented with the beauty of the sparkling lights of Pacitan City which look like a sprinkling of stars.
