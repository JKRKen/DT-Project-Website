Police arrested three perpetrators of motor vehicle theft in Tigaraksa, Tangerang, Banten. One of the perpetrators among the thieves is a drug dealer.

Tigaraksa Police Chief AKP Agus Ahmad Kurnia said the three perpetrators arrested had the initials D, aged 22, H, aged 34, and R, aged 22. The police arrested D on Monday, August 7 in Cikupa, Tangerang Regency after receiving a report that the motorcycle of a resident of Pasir Village Bolang, Tigaraksa District, Tangerang Regency, was stolen.

D admitted to the police that he had committed the theft with 2 of his friends. After obtaining this information, the police immediately moved to rented D, and there the police arrested 2 other perpetrators, namely H and R. After that, the police then conducted a search. The police found 21 plastic clip packs containing methamphetamine from R's bag.

Meanwhile, the Criminal Investigation Unit of the Tangerang Police, Kompol Arief Nazaruddin Yusuf, said that this case was still being developed. Development is carried out to reveal theft networks and drug dealer networks.
